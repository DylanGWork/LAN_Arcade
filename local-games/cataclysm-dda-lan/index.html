Should You Play?

Plain-language notes for someone deciding what to install or launch from the LAN Arcade.

Why play

This is deep, weird, punishing, and very offline-friendly. It can entertain one determined player for a long time.

Offline plan

Cache stable 0.I graphics/sounds packages plus terminal and Android options.

Expectation

It is not pick-up-and-play. The hub needs manuals and beginner guidance more than flashy marketing.
